# Chez Julien — Real Life Timeline vs Game Timeline

> This game is based on Julien's real experience running a cheese shop in Brussels (2022-2025). This document maps real events to game events for authenticity reference.

---

## The Real Story

Julien took over a bulk organic shop ("épicerie sèche") in Woluwe-Saint-Lambert, Brussels in July 2022. Over 3.5 years, he transformed it into a fine food / cheese shop called "Chez Julien." The game covers this entire journey.

## Real-Life Timeline Mapped to Game

### Year 1: July 2022 - June 2023 (Months 1-12)
- **July 2022**: Took over the shop. First decisions about Sunday opening, stock management.
- **August-October 2022**: Discovered bulk stock was dead weight. Started cheese pivot.
- **October 2022**: First raclette season discovery.
- **November 2022**: Insurance decisions. Early financial pressure.
- **December 2022**: First Christmas as shop owner (overwhelming, learning).
- **January-June 2023**: Gradual transformation from bulk to cheese. Building reputation.

### Year 2: July 2023 - June 2024 (Months 13-24)
- **July-August 2023**: Summer slowdown, heat waves.
- **September 2023**: Poncho adopted (Australian Shepherd).
- **October 2023**: Second raclette season (bigger, more varieties).
- **November 2023**: Madame Malfait calls about selling the building (€380k).
- **December 2023**: Christmas market opportunity. Building fund pressure begins.
- **January-June 2024**: Intense savings period for building purchase.

### Year 3: July 2024 - June 2025 (Months 25-36)
- **July 2024**: Building purchase deadline (need €80,000).
- **August 2024**: Extended deadline if needed.
- **September 2024**: Lucas joins as first employee.
- **October-November 2024**: Lucas settles in. Delegation learning.
- **December 2024**: Third Christmas (bigger operation now).
- **January-June 2025**: Post-building expenses accumulate (salary, car, apartment).

### Year 3.5: July 2025 - December 2025 (Months 37-42)
- **July-October 2025**: Late game. Reputation building. Producer visits.
- **November 2025**: Henry joins (Lucas's friend).
- **December 2025**: Final Christmas. Game ends.

## Key Decisions That Actually Happened

Based on the game design documents, these things ACTUALLY happened to Julien:
1. Opened on Sundays → led to burnout
2. Discovered cheese was the answer (Madame Dubois really existed)
3. Built up raclette as signature offering (became known for 15+ varieties)
4. Had the building purchase opportunity
5. Adopted Poncho (Australian Shepherd)
6. Hired Lucas (chaotic but beloved)
7. Hired Henry (reliable workhorse)
8. The shop IS called "Chez Julien"
9. Father actually was hospitalized
10. Multiple burnout episodes from overwork
