### Exterior Structure

- **Entrance Mats**: Two dark rectangular mats at door threshold

## üéØ Key Design Principles for Pixel Art

### 1. Facade Contrast
- **Dark shop panel** vs **light brickwork** = strong visual hierarchy
- Current pixel art shows brick everywhere - needs dark panel for shop front

### 2. Brick Color
- **Light tan/beige**, not dark brown
- Consistent pattern (no flickering)

### 3. Window Details
- **White frames** (not dark)
- **Stone sills** beneath windows
- **Bay window** protrusion on first floor
- Clear glass showing interior

### 4. Interior Richness
- **Dense cheese display** = visual indicator of success
- **Individual labels** = detail level for Stardew quality
- **Framed photos** = personal touch
- **Wine bottles** = clear indicator of `hasWineSelection`

### 5. Street Life
- **Wooden bench** = interactive element
- **Chalkboard** = dynamic signage
- **Bicycle** = charming detail
